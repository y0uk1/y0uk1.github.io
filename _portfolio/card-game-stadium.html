DESCRIPTION

This is a collection of exciting card games that includes popular classics such as Blackjack, Casino War, Speed, and Poker. Our platform is constantly evolving, and we are working on adding even more thrilling games, such as Texas Hold'em Poker and Rummy, to our repertoire. Our platform offers both logged in and guest user options. As a guest user, you can play any of the games available and your best scores will be saved for each game. However, if you choose to create an account, you can access additional features, including the ability to use your wallet to earn money in each game. Our platform also includes a leaderboard, where you can see how you stack up against other players in terms of the amount of money you've earned. With persistence and skill, you could become a millionaire in no time. Come join us and experience the thrill of these classic card games!

TECHNOLOGY

This game is implemented with range of powerful tools, including TypeScript, Next.js, Phaser3, Tailwind CSS, and Supabase. One of the key points of our platform is that each game extends from a common game scene, allowing for easy integration of new games in the future. This streamlined development approach enables us to add new games quickly and efficiently. I have primarily focused on developing Speed, Casino War, and Blackjack. To ensure the scalability and maintainability of our code, we've also implemented a robust class design, which enables us to manage complex game logic and maintain a consistent user experience across all games.
